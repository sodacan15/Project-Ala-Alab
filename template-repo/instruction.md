# Ala-Alab Structure and Verification Rules
*Preserving academic rigor and structural alignment across research repositories.*

## 📜 Core Structural Mandates
1. **Preserve Fixed Sections**: All logs must preserve the markdown headers exactly as provided in the `context.md` template.
2. **Inline Image Filename Rule**: Any mention of an image, photo, sketch, or map must include the exact inline filename (e.g., `water-height-gauge.png`) representing a physical asset placed in the repository's `/assets/` directory.
3. **Erratum vs Pivot Separation**: 
   - Factual corrections belong exclusively under the `## Erratum Log` (tagged as `[ERR-...]`).
   - Strategy or scope shifts belong exclusively under the `## Pivot Log` (tagged as `[PIVOT]`).
   - These are not interchangeable and are audited rigorously.
